Send magic packets to wake one or more computers via Wake-On-Lan.

WOL [device]
WOL [device1] [device2] ... [deviceN]
WOL /?

  device          MAC address of recipient device to send magic packets to.
                  Separate multiple recipient devices with spaces.
                  MAC addresses are accepted in the format 01-23-45-67-89-AB or
                  0123.4567.89AB, upper- or lower-case, using colons, hyphens 
                  or dots as separators.
  /?              Prints this help message.
